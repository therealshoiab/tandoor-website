## 💡 Why Your Restaurant Needs a Custom Website (3 Major Pain Points Solved)

1.  **Stop Paying Heavy Commission Fees (15-30%)**
    *   *The Problem:* Third-party delivery portals like Zomato and Swiggy eat up a massive chunk of your profits.
    *   *The Solution:* A direct WhatsApp ordering and table booking engine that cuts out the middleman.
2.  **Turn Social Media Followers into Paying Customers**
    *   *The Problem:* An Instagram profile alone doesn't let customers easily view a categorized menu, search for a dish, or make a reservation.
    *   *The Solution:* An interactive, lightning-fast digital menu with a direct "Order Now" call to action.
3.  **Stand Out from Competitors on Google Maps**
    *   *The Problem:* When customers search for "restaurants near me," they see identical Google listings.
    *   *The Solution:* A custom-designed website link on your Google profile raises your credibility, improves your local SEO rankings, and secures more table bookings.
